Overview

Debt Snowball Pro (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

Information We Collect

Debt Snowball Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All financial information entered into the app (such as debt balances, minimum payments, and interest rates) is stored locally on your device only.

We do not have access to your data.

Local Data Storage

All user-entered data is stored securely on the device using Apple’s local storage frameworks.

No data is transmitted to external servers.

No analytics or tracking services are used.

In-App Purchases

Premium features are processed securely through Apple’s StoreKit 2 framework.

We do not collect or store payment information.
All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple.

Please refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy for details on how Apple processes payment data.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with:
	•	Analytics platforms
	•	Advertising networks
	•	External APIs
	•	Cloud storage providers

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or distribute any user data.

Children’s Privacy

Debt Snowball Pro does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Control

Because all data is stored locally:
	•	Deleting the app removes all stored data.
	•	You may manually manage or remove your debts at any time within the app.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the “Last Updated” date.
